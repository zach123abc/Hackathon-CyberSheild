Password Safety Tips

Strong passwords are your first line of defense online. Here’s how to build one:

  • Use at least 12–16 characters.
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid personal info like your name, birthday, or common words.
  • Don’t reuse passwords across different accounts.
  • Use a password manager to keep track of complex passwords.

Example of a weak password: password123
Example of a strong password: K8!dYt#p4sQ@
