Password Safety Tips

Why Strong Passwords Matter

Weak passwords are the #1 reason accounts get hacked. Protect your digital life by using strong, unique passwords.

Best Practices

  • 12+ characters with mixed types
  • Passphrases instead of single words
  • Avoid personal info or dictionary words
  • Use different passwords for different accounts

Example Passwords

  • P@ssw0rd!NotEasy
  • MyDogEats2Much!
  • 9@dR$7vL!k2p
