Scam Awareness

Learn to spot and avoid online scams before they reach you.

Stay Alert

Scammers use manipulation to steal your personal information. Recognizing red flags early can protect you.

Common Scams

  • Phishing emails pretending to be trusted companies
  • Fake tech support calls or malicious popups
  • Social media impersonation and giveaway scams

Tips to Avoid Scams

  • Double-check the sender’s email address
  • Hover over links to preview URLs before clicking
  • Never share passwords or sensitive information
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be skeptical of urgency or fear tactics

CyberShield helps you recognize and avoid scams before they impact you.
