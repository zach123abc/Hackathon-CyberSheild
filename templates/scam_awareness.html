Scam Awareness

Stay Alert

Scammers try to trick you into giving sensitive info. Learn to identify red flags.

Common Scams

  • Phishing Emails pretending to be banks or services
  • Fake tech support calls or popups
  • Social media impersonations or giveaway scams

Tips to Avoid Scams

  • Check sender carefully
  • Hover over links before clicking
  • Never share passwords or sensitive info
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Be skeptical of urgent or fear-inducing messages

CyberShield helps you recognize and avoid scams before they impact you.
